# Melbourne CBD Business Establishments Analysis (2002–2023)


##  Project Overview
This independent data analysis project examines business establishment trends within the City of Melbourne from 2002 to 2023.
The objective is to understand long-term growth patterns, dominant industries, and structural changes in business activity using publicly available government data.

##  Business Questions
- Which Melbourne areas have the highest number of business establishments?
- How has total business activity changed from 2002 to 2023?
- Which industries dominate the region?
- Are there industries that are growing or declining significantly?
- What structural changes can be observed over time?

## Tools \& Technologies Used
- Python (Pandas for data analysis)
- Power BI (Dashboard \& visual storytelling)
- Excel (Initial data inspection)

##  Key Insights
- Total business establishments increased by 45.11% between 2002 and 2023.
- The data shows strong long-term growth with a temporary decline in 2022.
- Vacant space increased significantly over the period.
- Cafés and Restaurants are the leading active industry category.
- Service-based industries show consistent expansion.
